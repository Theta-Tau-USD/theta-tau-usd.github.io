About Our Chapter

Theta Tau is a co-ed professional engineering fraternity dedicated to developing and maintaining a high standard of professional interest among its members, and uniting them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.

At the University of San Diego, our chapter brings together engineering students from diverse backgrounds to create a supportive community focused on academic excellence, professional development, and meaningful service to our community.

Our Three Pillars

Brotherhood

Building lifelong bonds through shared experiences, mutual support, and a commitment to each other's success. Our brotherhood extends beyond graduation, creating a network of engineers who support one another throughout their careers.

  • Social events and bonding activities
  • Mentorship programs
  • Lifelong network of professional engineers

Service

Dedicating our engineering skills and knowledge to serve our campus, local community, and society. We believe in using our talents to make a positive impact and give back to those around us.

  • Community outreach programs
  • STEM education initiatives
  • Campus and local service projects

Professionalism

Developing the skills, knowledge, and ethics needed to excel in engineering careers. We provide opportunities for professional growth through workshops, networking events, and industry connections.

  • Professional development workshops
  • Industry networking events
  • Career mentorship and guidance
